Meeting: SOC 2 & Security Posture
Date: 2025-10-14
Attendees: Sara (CEO), Marc (CTO), David (Eng Lead)

Marc: Two enterprise prospects asked for SOC 2 reports last week. We don't
have one. We need to decide if and when we go for it.

Sara: What's the realistic timeline?

Marc: Type 1 takes two to three months from kickoff. Type 2 needs six months
of observation period on top of that. So if we start now, Type 1 by January,
Type 2 by July.

David: And the cost?

Marc: Vanta or Drata for the automation, around 18k a year. Auditor fees on
top, another 15 to 20k for Type 1. So roughly 35k all in for the first year.

Sara: How many deals are we losing without it?

Marc: At least two right now. By Q1 it'll be most enterprise deals. SOC 2 is
table stakes above a certain deal size.

Sara: Then it's not really a decision. We start now. Decision: we pursue SOC
2 Type 1, kickoff this month, target completion end of January. Marc owns
vendor selection, I own the budget approval.

David: Vanta or Drata?

Marc: I'll do a one-week eval. They're roughly equivalent, Drata has slightly
better automated evidence collection, Vanta has a more mature partner
ecosystem.

Sara: Make the call by next week. Don't drag it.

Marc: One more thing. SOC 2 will force us to formalize a few things — access
review process, vendor management, incident response runbook. David, that's
going to land partly on engineering.

David: Expected. I'll allocate Karim 20 percent for the next quarter on
compliance enablement.

Sara: Good. Move on.
