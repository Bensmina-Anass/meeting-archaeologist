Meeting: Q4 Product Roadmap
Date: 2025-10-07
Attendees: Sara (CEO), Léa (PM), Marc (CTO), Yann (Sales)

Léa: I've consolidated the Q4 roadmap based on customer interviews and what
sales is hearing. Three big rocks: SSO and SAML for enterprise, an audit log
feature, and a basic reporting dashboard.

Yann: SSO is the one I keep getting blocked on. Three deals are stuck behind
it right now.

Marc: Auth0 gives us SSO and SAML essentially for free, we just need to wire
up the UI for tenant configuration. That's maybe two weeks of frontend work.

Sara: Audit log?

Léa: Enterprise compliance requirement. Every action a user takes in the
system needs to be logged with timestamp, actor, and resource. Searchable for
at least 90 days.

Marc: Three weeks of work. We need a separate write path that doesn't slow
down the main request flow.

Sara: Reporting dashboard?

Léa: Customers want to see usage, active users, key workflows completed. It's
the lightest of the three but it's also the one that drives expansion
conversations because once they see usage they want more seats.

Sara: Sequencing?

Léa: SSO first because it unblocks revenue. Audit log second because it
unblocks the next tier of deals. Dashboard third.

Marc: I can put two engineers on SSO immediately.

Sara: Decision: Q4 priorities are SSO, audit log, reporting dashboard, in
that order. Léa owns the spec for each, Marc owns delivery. Target: SSO live
by end of October, audit log mid-November, dashboard by mid-December.

Yann: I'll tell the three stuck deals SSO is end of October.

Sara: Tell them early November to be safe. Don't promise the aggressive date.
